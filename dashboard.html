Geethanjali Institute of Technology, Nellore

About Us

Geethanjali Institute of Technology, Nellore is committed to providing quality education and fostering an environment of innovation and learning. Established in 2008, our institute offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in engineering, management, and technology.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education, innovation, and research, shaping future leaders and entrepreneurs who contribute to society with ethical values and technological advancements.

Courses Offered

B.Tech in Computer Science

Learn about software development, algorithms, data structures, and cutting-edge technologies.

B.Tech in Electronics and Communication

Explore the world of communication systems, embedded systems, and VLSI design.

MBA in Technology Management

Combine technical knowledge with management skills to lead technology-driven organizations.

Campus Life

Our campus offers a vibrant environment for students, including state-of-the-art laboratories, sports facilities, and cultural activities. Join us and experience a holistic education that balances academics with extracurriculars.
